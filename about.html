"That there is an immense variety in global problems almost defying generalization cannot be denied, although all are capable of being resolved if only the talent available in the international community...were to be harnessed for the task."
-Fr. Thomas Cowley

EPIC is a faith-based service organization. It works with individuals and grassroots organizations who are implementing creative solutions to serious problems.

EPIC Founder, Fr. Thomas Cowley, felt that a key to solving the world's problems was encouraging and enabling talented and committed individuals and local organizations.
This enables a small US non- profit like EPIC to have a significant impact on numerous development projects across the globe.
