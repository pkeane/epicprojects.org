Oxfam: Haiti Relief
Disaster Relief and Reconstruction
Haiti

After the January 2010 earthquake in Haiti, EPIC members snapped into action raising thousands of dollars for relief. A donation was made to Oxfam USA for sanitation and water in refugee camps. EPIC is very proud of our members for their generous contributions toward immediate and long-term construction

EPIC members continues to support Oxfam today as much is still left to be done.

Visit Oxfam's website for updates on their efforts in Haiti and other projects
