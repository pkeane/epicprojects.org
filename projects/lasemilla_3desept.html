Agricultural Training Center
La Semilla del Progreso
Honduras

The 3 de Septiembre Community

Ten years ago Honduran land reform made this exhausted piece of land available to the 3 de Septiember community. La Semilla Progreso provided agricultural classes for the group and continued guidence to make it productive. They have been able to improve the soil's fertility and increase production.

The group was able to purchase a sugar cane press with a loan from the Dunamis Peace Institute which was paid off in 2011. The sugar cane press will enable them to make more money from what they have grown.
